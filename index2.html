    Empowering health equity
    Research and advocacy for all

    At Health Equity and NTDs Research Institute, Ghana, we are dedicated to advancing population health and health equity across communities. Our focus on neglected tropical diseases (NTDs) aligns with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), driving initiatives that safeguard the wellbeing of individuals and families. Through rigorous research and proactive policy advocacy, we strive to influence Ghana's development positively. Join us in our mission to create a healthier, equitable future for all.
